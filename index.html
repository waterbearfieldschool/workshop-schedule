Workshop Descriptions

Intro to Mesh Radio

Beginner-Friendly

Learn how to build and deploy your own mesh radio node using LoRa technology. Mesh networks let communities communicate even when cell towers and internet are down - perfect for emergency preparedness, outdoor adventures, or just staying connected with neighbors.

What You'll Learn

  • How mesh radio networks work and why they matter
  • Setting up and configuring a Meshtastic node
  • Connecting with other nodes in your area
  • Best practices for range and reliability

Take Home

Optional: Purchase a ready-to-use radio node kit for $30 to take home and join the local mesh network.

Intro to Off-Grid Telemetry

Beginner-Friendly

Build sensors to monitor temperature, humidity, soil moisture, and more - all without relying on cloud services or monthly fees. Perfect for greenhouses, root cellars, beehives, chicken coops, or anywhere you need to keep an eye on conditions.

What You'll Learn

  • Sensor basics: what to measure and why
  • Setting up low-power monitoring devices
  • Viewing your data locally or over mesh radio
  • Practical applications for farms, gardens, and homes

Cold Frames & Hoop Houses

Beginner-Friendly

Extend your growing season by weeks or even months with simple, low-cost structures. Cold frames and hoop houses protect plants from frost and create microclimates that let you start seeds earlier and harvest later into the fall.

What You'll Learn

  • Design principles for passive solar heating
  • Building a cold frame from recycled materials
  • Hoop house construction basics
  • Ventilation and temperature management

Take Home

Plans and materials list to build your own cold frame at home.
